THE BAND

OUR STORY

Many Faces began with Moses, the band’s guitarist, and Ben, the bassist, who have been close friends since childhood. After playing together in various bands, they formed their first major project, Mindrive, a glam/alternative rock band. Despite their hard work and countless performances, internal conflicts led to the band's disbandment. Seeking a fresh perspective, Moses spent six months in India to clear his mind, and later moved to the U.S. for nearly four years, where he wrote a significant amount of music. Throughout this time, Moses and Ben stayed in touch, sharing ideas and discussing the possibility of starting a new project once Moses returned to Israel. Upon his return, they decided to form an Alternative Metal band and began working on the material Moses had written.

The duo started to build the concept for their debut album, Krifignos, and initially brought in Yuval (vocals) and Eshchar (drums) to round out the lineup. However, due to personal reasons, both Yuval and Eshchar eventually had to part ways with the band. Despite this setback, Moses and Ben continued to develop the album on their own, with the songs slowly taking on a Progressive Metal vibe. After a period of searching, they discovered Lior, whose voice perfectly matched their vision. Lior joined the band, recording their track Infinity in a single take, and solidified his place as their vocalist. Today, Many Faces is hard at work on their debut album, Krifignos, a Progressive/Alternative Metal record that will feature 11 tracks. The band is excited to share their music with the world and continue their musical journey.



KRIFIGNOS


Krifignos is the debut album that Many Faces is currently working on. It's a culmination of years of musical exploration, personal growth, and collaboration. The album will feature a unique blend of progressive metal with rich melodies, poetic lyrics, and a touch of experimental soundscapes. With themes that explore identity, self-discovery, and the human experience, Krifignos promises to deliver a powerful emotional journey for its listeners. Stay tuned for its release!
