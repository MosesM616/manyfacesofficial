OUR STORY

Many Faces was forged from the longstanding partnership of guitarist Moses and bassist Ben, whose shared history dates back to childhood. After years of collaboration in various projects, they formed Mindrive, a glam/alternative rock band. Despite relentless effort and countless performances, internal tensions led to its disbandment.

Upon Moses' return to Israel, the duo set the stage for a new venture—an Alternative Metal project that would evolve into their debut album, Krifignos. Initial members, Yuval (vocals) and Eshchar (drums), parted ways due to personal reasons, but the vision endured. With the songs taking on a more Progressive Metal edge, the search for a voice to match their sound led to Lior. His arrival was pivotal—he recorded Infinity in a single take, solidifying his place as frontman.

Now, Many Faces is set to release Krifignos, a haunting and complex Progressive Metal album. With 11 tracks that delve into shadowed realms, the band is ready to share their vision with the world and continue their relentless journey.

The Sound of Many Faces

A Journey Through Light & Darkness

The music of Many Faces is a powerful fusion of searing guitars, relentless rhythms, and dark melodies that search for truth beyond the light. Our sound draws inspiration from a wide array of sub-genres, reinventing the boundaries between progressive, alternative, and classic metal. Every note we play places us at the intersection of sound and darkness—music that leaves its mark through time, burning a path and inviting the listener to join the journey.
Our musical style knows no limits: from lyrics buried beneath layers of emotion to guitar riffs that carve out new landscapes of auditory experience. With Krifignos, our debut album, we are not just telling a story—we are defining a new realm of metal and alternative that resonates with anyone ready to confront the shadows within.


KRIFIGNOS



Krifignos is the debut album that Many Faces is currently working on. It's a culmination of years of musical exploration, personal growth, and collaboration. The album will feature a unique blend of progressive metal with rich melodies, poetic lyrics, and a touch of experimental soundscapes. With themes that explore identity, self-discovery, and the human experience, Krifignos promises to deliver a powerful emotional journey for its listeners. Stay tuned for its release!
